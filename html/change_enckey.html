Change Password (Encryption Key)

Here, you can change the encryption key associated with your name.

Important: the encryption key is used to encrypt and decrypt your mailbox. Currently, you can change your encryption key, but your mailbox will NOT be re-encrypted with the new key. Instead, it will be deleted and re-created using the new key. Which means you will lose all the existing messages in your IMAP mailbox. Consider this before proceeding!
