Ubikom: digital avatars on blockchain

lgx@ubikom.cc; sasha@ubikom.cc

Digital avatars are user-chosen online identities. An avatar consists of a name and public key, which is used for public key cryptography. Avatars are created, owned, and managed by users.

Ubikom is a decentralized infrastructure of digital avatars. Avatars are registered on Ubikom’s blockchain. This leads to superior data privacy, resistance to censorship, secure communication, and convenient online authentication.

Since avatars are freely chosen by users and not tied to real identities, Ubikom avoids the bureaucratic obstacles plaguing Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI)

There are are numerous applications of the decentralized avatar registry. Our first focus is on creating secure decentralized email. All Ubikom emails are end-to-end encrypted with the algorithms that secure Bitcoin. Even if your emails are intercepted, cryptography makes it impossible to read their content. All Ubikom emails are encrypted by default, so that users don’t need to worry about confidentiality.

Moreover, the decentralized registry is immune to censorship and incorruptible. The integrity of records is protected by blockchain technology. No state or corporate actor can ban anyone from using Ubikom.

Ubikom is for you if you are worried about privacy of your data, are concerned about Big Brother (either a government or corporation) spying on your email, live under an authoritarian or oppressive regime, run a business that requires confidentiality of communication, or looking for a robust, convenient and customer-friendly solution for a regulated agency or a health care organization.
